What Companion is

Companion is a personal, consent-based supportive wellness text-messaging program. The texting persona is named “Ava.” Ava sends short, encouraging, no-pressure check-ins by SMS and replies when you write back. It is a quiet way to feel a little less alone — not a product, not a promotion, and not a substitute for professional or emergency care.

The plain facts

  • Who sends the messages Dimitri Restaino, operating as a sole proprietor. This is a personal, non-commercial program he runs himself.
  • Who receives them Only a small number of individuals Dimitri personally knows who have opted in. We do not buy, rent, or use marketing lists, and we never add anyone without their own consent.
  • Why To offer supportive, encouraging, two-way wellness check-ins. The goal is connection and encouragement — there is nothing to buy and nothing being marketed.
  • What it is not Not marketing, not promotional, no third parties, no affiliates, no purchased lists, and not medical or emergency care.

How to opt in

There are two ways to start, and both require your own clear consent:

  • Visit the opt-in page, enter your mobile number, and check the consent box.
  • Or, if you are already in touch with the program, reply JOIN to a message to confirm you want to receive recurring check-ins.

You are always in control: reply STOP at any time to opt out, or HELP for help.
