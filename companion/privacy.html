Privacy Policy

Program: Companion · Operated by Dimitri Restaino (Dimitri Cole), sole proprietor · Last updated June 25, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Companion (“Companion,” “we,” “us”) — a personal, consent-based supportive wellness text-messaging program operated by Dimitri Restaino as a sole proprietor — collects and uses information from individuals who have opted in to receive our messages. Companion is a personal, non-marketing program offered to a small number of people Dimitri personally knows.

Information we collect

We collect only what is needed to operate the program:

  • Your mobile phone number.
  • Your consent record — the exact opt-in language you agreed to, plus the date, time, and basic technical details (such as your browser’s user agent) of your submission.
  • The content of the text messages you exchange with the program.

We do not collect location data, financial information, or data from any source other than your own opt-in and messages.

How we use your information

We use your phone number and messages solely to operate the program you opted into: to send supportive wellness check-ins, respond to your messages, and maintain the context of your conversation over time. We keep your consent record to document that you opted in. We do not use your information for advertising or marketing of any kind.

How we share your information — mobile opt-in data is never shared for marketing

Mobile opt-in information, your phone number, and your consent are NOT shared with any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. We do not sell, rent, or trade your information. No mobile information is shared with third parties for their own marketing.

We rely on a small number of trusted service providers strictly to operate the program — a messaging carrier to deliver SMS and a messaging/AI provider to help generate replies. They process your information only to provide that functionality on our behalf, never for their own marketing, and they are not permitted to share it onward for marketing.

Data retention and deletion

We retain your messages and consent record only as long as needed to operate the program and to document your opt-in. You may request that we delete your information at any time by contacting us at the email below.

Opting out

You can stop receiving messages at any time by replying STOP. For help, reply HELP or contact us at the email below. Message frequency varies, and message and data rates may apply.
